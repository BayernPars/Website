    About US

    The official fanclub of FC Bayern for the Persian People. A Huge group in the name of "FC Bayern Middle East fans #FCBParsi has started strongly on 04 jan. 2014 to cover the Persian-speaking countries in the Middle East and the countries that have familiarity with Persian language. In such a short time the fanclub recieved a great support and welcome due to different reasons to include geographically speard-out speakers and mutual culture, lack of fan club in some of the countries such as Afghanistan, Tajikistan ...

    FCBParsi will try to bring all the members together who are in the following countries; Iran, Afghanistan, Armenia, Azerbaijan, Uzbekistan and some regions of Iraq, Pakistan and India in which Persian is still spoken. Besides these countries, we have representatives who are immigrants in several countries in Europe and America. As one of our important activities regarding supporting fans, we have registered friendship treaties with other fan clubs from across the world. There is communications and friendly agreements between us and other fan clubs in all over the world. It will help the fans from both groups to discuss the football matters closely or help with other non-fan subjects such as finding hotels and etc. These agreements show the sense of humanity and unity.

    Appreciate to your support and help, we can celebrate both days of fan-group founded day on persian new year (norouz) and officialize day on AD new year.
